# Memo Template

## 1) Executive Summary

- Company / ticker / filing window reviewed
- Overall risk level: Low / Moderate / High
- Most material concerns (max 5)

## 2) Risk Register

For each item:

- `Risk ID`
- `Category` (`EMx`, `CFx`, `KMx`, `AAx`)
- `Hypothesis`
- `Evidence IDs`
- `Confidence`
- `Potential valuation impact`
- `What would falsify this view`

## 3) Cross-Period Changes

- What changed vs prior quarter/year
- Whether trend strengthens or weakens each risk hypothesis

## 4) Diligence Questions for Management / IR / Audit Committee

- Question
- Why it matters
- Which risk it addresses

## 5) Monitoring Plan

- Metric / disclosure trigger
- Update frequency
- Escalation condition
